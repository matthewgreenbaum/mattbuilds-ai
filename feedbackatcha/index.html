FAQ

What is FeedbackAtcha?

FeedbackAtcha is an AI-powered tool that helps B2B SaaS marketers craft professional, actionable creative feedback. Think of it as the experienced colleague who always knows exactly what to say in a creative review.

Who is this for?

Marketing managers, directors, and VPs who review creative work regularly — ads, emails, landing pages, decks, videos, and more. Whether you're giving feedback to designers, copywriters, agencies, or cross-functional teams.

What are the three modes?

Full Read gives you a situation assessment plus a complete feedback draft. Quick Take is a sharp one-liner followed by the draft. Just Draft It skips the preamble and goes straight to polished feedback.

What about my brand guidelines?

Use the Brand Profile panel in the sidebar to add your website URL, brand guidelines (paste text, upload a PDF, or link to a doc), and brand colors. When enabled, your brand context is woven into every piece of feedback.

Is my data private?

FeedbackAtcha runs entirely in your browser. Your brand settings are stored locally on your device. Nothing is sent to any server other than the Anthropic API to generate feedback.

1. Share what you received

Paste the copy, upload the creative (image, PDF, deck), or just describe what your team sent you.

2. Add context

Expand the "Add details" panel to tag the asset type, the stage of the creative, your audience, and the goal. The more context you give, the sharper the feedback.

3. Pick a mode

Full Read for a thorough review, Quick Take for a fast one-liner plus draft, or Just Draft It when you already know the direction and just need polished feedback to send.

4. Send it back

Copy the feedback, tweak anything that needs your personal touch, and paste it into Slack, email, or your review doc. Every feedback point is tagged [CRITICAL], [CAUTION], or [GOOD] so your team knows what to prioritize.

Brand compliance

When your Brand Profile is active, FeedbackAtcha compares every asset you review against your guidelines — flagging color, typography, voice, and logo deviations explicitly.

Roadmap notes

FeedbackAtcha is an ongoing build. Current focus areas: richer brand-compliance checks, saved review history, and multi-asset comparison. Have an idea? Send it to matt via the portfolio contact.

Known quirks

Very large PDFs can slow down the initial upload. Images work best at 2MB or under. If feedback ever feels generic, add more context in the "Add details" panel — the model is only as sharp as the brief.
